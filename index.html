How I think

Builder's mindset,
PM's compass

The PM in me thinks in strategy and outcomes. The builder in me ships working demos in weeks. AI is the amplifier between both.

01 — Approach
Ship prototypes, not slide decks
Instead of waiting months for production-ready builds, I deliver functional, demoable MVPs directly to leadership and sales engineers. Validate fast, iterate smarter — agile product development amplified by AI.
02 — Edge
Bridging product & engineering
I speak both languages fluently. I can sit with architects on system design decisions then translate the same nuance into a compelling customer story — without losing fidelity on either side.
03 — Philosophy
"I don't wait months for a dev team. I build the first version myself — and use that to get everyone aligned."
Fail fast. Learn faster. Ship smarter.
Delivering 5 AI use cases in 6 months wasn't luck — it was a methodology. Prototype in public, gather feedback early, and only commit engineering resources once the concept is validated in front of real customers.
04 — Leadership
Customer-first, always
Strong communication, storytelling, and the ability to align cross-functional teams around a single, clear outcome. I don't just define what to build — I make sure every stakeholder understands why.
05 — AI-First
AI as leverage, not novelty
GitHub Copilot, Claude Code, Cursor AI, Azure AI Foundry — these aren't tools I demo. They're how I work every day. AI removes the bottlenecks between ideation and working software.
